About Us

Welcome to The Checkered Flag Slot Cars & RC Complex, Sevierville's premier destination for slot car and RC racing enthusiasts. Our state-of-the-art facility features multiple tracks, a fully-stocked pro shop, and a community of passionate racers. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or just looking for family fun, we have something for everyone!
