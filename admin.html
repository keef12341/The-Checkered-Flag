About Us

Welcome to The Checkered Flag, the premier destination for slot car and RC enthusiasts! Our state-of-the-art facility offers multiple tracks, a fully stocked shop, and a community of passionate racers.

Founded in 2010, we've been serving racing enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to try slot car racing for the first time or a seasoned pro preparing for competition, we have everything you need.

Our friendly staff is always ready to help with tips, repairs, and advice to enhance your racing experience.
